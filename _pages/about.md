I am a political science Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Political Science at Emory University. I study local politics and research methods, with a concentration on the politics of policing, public policy, and data quality. My dissertation project focuses on hiring practices of police departments and their downstream consequences on police performance and public trust.

Prior to joining Emory University, I earned my B.S. in Economics from the Georgia Institute of Technology in 2021.
